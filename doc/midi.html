Midi Extension

The Midi Extension for NetLogo provides primitives to add sound to NetLogo models. It supports full access to the MIDI interface.

midi:pitch.bend

midi:pitch.bend channel bend

Pitch Bend messages tell the receiving device to change the pitch of a sound. These messages are usually sent by moving a lever or wheel on the left side of a MIDI keyboard.

Fine tuning to volume

midi:modulation

midi:modulation channel modulation

Modulation is usually used to control vibrato, it can usually be assigned to other parameters such as pitch and volume.

midi:pan

midi:pan channel pan

Where within the stereo field the device's sound will be placed (assuming that it has stereo audio outputs). If a MultiTimbral device, then each Part usually has its own pan position. This is generally when Pan becomes useful, because then you can use Pan, Volume, and Balance controllers to internally mix all of the Parts to the device's stereo outputs. Pan should effect all notes on the channel, including notes that were triggered prior to the pan message being received, and are still sustaining.
